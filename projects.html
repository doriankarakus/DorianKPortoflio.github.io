Projects

Familiar Faces Across the Galaxy

(October 2021-December 2021)

Utilized DFS and BFS search algorithms to find links between artificially created users via mutual friends simulated a way for friends to meet mutual friends within social media sites such as Facebook. As part of a small group, I didn’t have a specific set of responsibilities but worked with my team to organize and complete steps towards the project’s development.

Study Buddy

(February 2022-May 2022)

Part of a team that created an interactive character (“Buddy”) that users could interact and play with to take needed study breaks using SDL2, Windows API and our UI, a user could play with (“Buddy”) and set the timer easily. My role in this project was centered around timer functionality, assistance with Buddy’s character development and organization of tasks of all members.

LOCUS TECHNOLOGY, LLC

(April 2022- February 2024)

Co-creator/owner in partnership creating devices for the visually impaired using ultrasonic technology incorporating Arduino, Seeeduino, and other microprocessor products. I took a major role in coding and provided an equal contribution to the physical electro-mechanical development. Our combined efforts led to Locus Technology LLC being awarded as a Top 21 National Finalist for the Cade Prize in August 2023.

VibeTunes

(September 2023 - December 2023)

As a member of a three-person development team, I contributed to the creation of a mood-based music generation platform, which curates personalized playlists aligned with the user's selected mood. The project employed the ReactJS framework in conjunction with the Spotify Web API to access and modify users' Spotify playlists. My primary responsibility involved leading the frontend design and implementation. Additionally, I conducted user testing and performed a comprehensive analysis of user data collected from our peers, facilitating enhancements to address the application's deficiencies.

ChronoQuest

(February 2024 - May 2024)

As a member of a four-person development team, we successfully created an engaging and challenging 2D platformer utilizing the Godot engine. My responsibilities within this collaborative effort encompassed a diverse range of tasks essential to the game's development lifecycle. These tasks included initial game design conceptualization, digital asset aggregation, development of fundamental movement mechanics and physics interactions, creation of multiple level designs, and various supplementary activities.
